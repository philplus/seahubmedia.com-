# Screening Rubric (fast pre-screen)

## Status flow
New → Contacted → Replied → Screening Call → Interview 1 → Interview 2 → Reference → Offer → Hired / Rejected

## Finance Accounting (1) | 6–8 juta | D3+ | EN required, CN preferred
Hard pass:
- ≥1 year accounting/finance work experience
- Hands-on journal entry / bookkeeping (AP/AR/GL) experience
- English working ability (email/report)
Nice-to-have:
- E-commerce reconciliation (platform payouts, refunds, ads spend)
- Excel/Google Sheets (pivot/VLOOKUP), basic SQL a plus
Red flags:
- Only “admin” without accounting entries
- Weak English comprehension

## HR (3) | 5–8 juta | e-commerce or beauty/personal care exp preferred
Hard pass:
- HR experience in beauty/personal care OR e-commerce company
Nice-to-have:
- Recruiting for ops/marketing/BD roles
- Payroll/admin experience, basic labor compliance

## HR Leader (1) | 8–20 juta | EN required, CN preferred
Hard pass:
- Proven end-to-end HR leadership (recruiting + policy + ER)
- English fluent/working
Nice-to-have:
- Built teams in e-commerce/beauty; strong hiring pipeline

## Creator BD / Creator Manager (5) | 5–10 juta | EN not required | KPI: #creators + results
Hard pass:
- Influencer/creator partnership / affiliate / BD experience
- Has real cases: #creators onboarded, GMV/ROI outcomes
Nice-to-have:
- TikTok Shop Affiliate familiarity, agency/MCN network
Red flags:
- Only “social media admin” without partnership numbers

## Creator BD Leader (1) | 8–12 juta | EN optional | manages BD team
Hard pass:
- Managed BD/creator team OR clear senior ownership of pipeline
- Can design SOP + weekly targets + coaching
Nice-to-have:
- Beauty/personal care creator network
